Our Services

  • Facilitating Artists of All Genres

    We provide a creative environment for artists to compose and produce music, regardless of genre.

  • Music Composition

    We compose music for commercials, jingles, teledramas, films, and other broadcast productions.

  • Multi-Track Live Simultaneous Recording

    Using innovative techniques, we are capable of recording live bands, orchestras, and more.

  • Virtual Studio Technology (VST)

    We offer endless possibilities to create great sound with live instruments and VST.

  • Digital Mixing and Mastering

    Our state-of-the-art digital mixing and mastering services utilize Cubase, FL Studio, Wavelab, and Sound Forge.

  • Audio Restoration and Post-Production

    We specialize in the restoration and post-production of audio to ensure the highest quality sound.
